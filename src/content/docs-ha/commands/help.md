# Help

Yana buɗe panel na taimako a cikin manhaja tare da taƙaitawar dukkan gajerun hanyoyin madannai, shawarwarin kewayawa, da amfani gaba ɗaya.

## Yadda za a yi amfani

Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Help** ko rubuta `help` a tashar umarni. Panel na taimako yana buɗewa nan take kuma umarnin yana fita.

## Abin da ke cikin panel na taimako

### Kewayawa
| Gajeriyar hanya | Aiki |
|----------|--------|
| `Scroll wheel` | Kara/rage zoom |
| `Middle click + drag` | Motsa kallon |
| `Double middle click` | Sanya dukkan abubuwa cikin allo |

### Umarni
| Gajeriyar hanya | Aiki |
|----------|--------|
| Rubutu | Fara rubuta sunan umarni — shawarwari suna bayyana a kan canvas |
| `Delete` | Share dukkan abubuwan da aka zaɓa |
| `Enter` / `Space` | Tabbatar da umarni ko shigarwa |
| `Enter` / `Space` (ba a aiki) | Sake yin umarnin ƙarshe da aka yi amfani da shi |
| `Escape` | Soke umarnin na yanzu ko wanke zaɓi |

### Zaɓi
| Gajeriyar hanya | Aiki |
|----------|--------|
| `Left click` | Zaɓi abu |
| `Drag →` | Zaɓi kawai abubuwan da suke **cikakke cikin** yankin ja |
| `Drag ←` | Zaɓi kowane abu da ke **taɓawa** yankin ja |
| `Left click grip` | Gyara vertex ko ƙarshe kai tsaye |

### Shawarwarin zane
- **Tabon snap** — mai nuni yana snap zuwa ƙarshen abubuwan da ke kusa kai tsaye.
- **Kusurwa mai dorewa** — motsa mai nuni nesa isasshe a shugabanci ɗaya don kulle zuwa matakan 45°.
- **Tsawo madaidaici** — yayin da kusurwar aka kulle, rubuta lamba ka danna `Enter` don saita nisa madaidaici.
