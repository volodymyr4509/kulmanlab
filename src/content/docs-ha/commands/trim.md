# Trim

Umarnin `trim` yana cire sashen [Line](../line/) wanda ke tsakanin tabon mahaɗa biyu makwabta, yana rabe layin zuwa sashi ɗaya ko biyu masu guntu. Ana tantance sashen da za a yanke ta matsayin mai nuni — riƙe a kan sashen da kake son cirewa ka danna don yanke ta.

Trim yana aiki akan **abubuwan Line kaɗai**. Ga baka, da'irori, polylines, da wasu nauʼukan abu, yi amfani da [Delete](../delete/) ko gyaran grip maimakon.

## Yanke layi

1. Rubuta `trim` a tashar umarni ko danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Trim**.
2. **Riƙe a kan sashen layi** da kake son cirewa — preview mai ja yana haskaka daidai sashen da za a yanke.
3. **Danna** don cire wannan sashi.

Umarnin yana ci gaba da zama a aiki bayan kowace yankewa, don haka za ka iya ci gaba da riƙe da dannawa don yanke ƙarin sassa. Danna **Escape** don fita.

```
  Kafin:                     Bayan yanke sashen tsakiya:

  ──────●──────●──────        ──────●          ●──────
      mahaɗa    mahaɗa       (bangaren hagu)  (bangaren dama)
                              (sashen tsakiya an cire)
```

## Yadda ake tantance sashen yankewa

Umarnin yana projekta matsayin mai nuni a kan layin da aka riƙe kuma yana samun dukkan tabon mahaɗa da layin ke da su tare da wasu abubuwa. Waɗannan sigogin mahaɗa suna rabe layin zuwa sassa. Ana haskaka sashin da tsakaninsa ke ɗauke da projeciyar mai nuni kuma za a cire shi a dannawa.

- Idan mai nuni yana **kafin mahaɗa na farko**: ana cire wancan bangaren farko na layin.
- Idan mai nuni yana **tsakanin mahaɗa biyu**: ana cire wannan bangaren tsakiya; layin yana rabe zuwa biyu.
- Idan mai nuni yana **bayan mahaɗa na ƙarshe**: ana cire wancan bangaren ƙarshe.
- Idan layin **babu mahaɗa** tare da wani abu daban: babu preview da ke bayyana kuma dannawa ba ya yin komai.

## Marfe na maɓallan madannai

| Maɓalli | Aiki |
|-----|--------|
| `Escape` | Fita daga yanayin trim |

## Abubuwan da ake goyon baya

| Abu | Ana Iya Yankewa? |
|--------|----------------|
| Line | Eh |
| Arc, Circle, Ellipse | Aʼa |
| Polyline / Rectangle | Aʼa |
| Text, Spline, Dimension, Leader | Aʼa |

Abubuwan da ake amfani da su a matsayin **iyakokin yankewa** za su iya kasancewa kowace nauʼi — kawai layin da za a yanke dole ne ya kasance abin Line.

## Trim da Extend

| | Trim | Extend |
|---|------|--------|
| Abin da yake yi | Yana cire sashen layi | Yana tsawaita ƙarshen layi zuwa iyaka |
| Kunnawa | Riƙe a kan sashen don yanke | Riƙe kusa da ƙarshen don tsawaita |
| Sakamako | Layin yana rabuwa ko guntuwa | Ƙarshen layi yana motsawa zuwa iyaka |
| Duka biyun | Lines kaɗai | Lines kaɗai |
