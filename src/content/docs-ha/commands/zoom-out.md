# Zoom Out

Umarnin `zoomout` yana rabe matakin zoom na yanzu da **1.5×** (daidai da ninka da ~0.667) kuma yana fita nan take, an ma tsakiya a tsakiyar viewport. Wannan shine adawar [Zoom In](../zoom-in/).

## Rage girman kallon

Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Zoom Out** ko rubuta `zoomout` a tashar umarni. Ana amfani da zoom nan take kuma umarnin yana fita — ba a bukatar dannawa a kan canvas.

## Yadda mataki na 1.5× ke aiki

| Zoom na yanzu | Bayan Zoom Out ɗaya |
|-------------|-------------------|
| 1.50× | 1.00× |
| 2.25× | 1.50× |
| 10.00× | 6.67× |
| 0.015× | 0.01× (an kwaɓe zuwa ƙasa) |

Matakin zoom koyaushe ana nuna shi a **kusurwa dama-ƙasa** na canvas. Iyaka mafi ƙasa **0.01×**; matakai ƙari ba sa yin komai.

## Zoom Out na kayan aiki da gilashin gilma

| | Maɓallin Zoom Out | Gilashin gilma |
|---|----------------|-------------|
| Tsakiyar zoom | Tsakiyar viewport | Matsayin mai nuni |
| Girman mataki | 1.5× ga kowace dannawa | ~1.1× ga kowane tik |
| Yana bukatar kunnawa | Aʼa | Aʼa — yana aiki koyaushe |
| Mafi kyau don | Komawa baya don ganin ƙarin fage | Zoom-waje mai santsi mai maida hankali kan mai nuni |

## Marfe na maɓallan madannai

Babu gajerun hanyoyin madannai ga wannan umarni. Yi amfani da gilashin gilma maimakon — yana aiki a kowane lokaci ba tare da kunna wani umarni ba.
