# Files

Umarnin **Files** yana buɗe panel na **Fayilolin Kwanan Nan** — jerin dukkan zanen-zane da aka ajiye a ajiyar gida ta burauzarka. Yi amfani da shi don sake buɗe zane na baya ko cire fayilolin da ba ka bukata su ba.

## Yadda za a buɗe panel na Fayilolin Kwanan Nan

Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Files** (alamar tarihi) a panel na File a saman allo. Panel yana buɗewa a gefen hagu na canvas.

## Panel na Fayilolin Kwanan Nan

Kowace shigarwa a jerin tana nuna sunan fayil da maɓallan aiki guda biyu:

| Maɓalli | Aiki |
|--------|--------|
| ✓ **Mayar da** | Loda zanen zuwa canvas — yana maye gurbin abin da ke ciki na yanzu |
| ✕ **Share** | Cire fayil daga ajiyar burauza na dindindin |

Idan babu fayilolin da aka ajiye tukuna, panel yana nuna "No files saved".

## Yadda ake ajiye fayiloli

KulmanLab yana ajiye zanen-zane a **IndexedDB** — bayanan gida mai dorewa a cikin burauza. Wannan yana nufin:

- Fayiloli suna wanzuwa bayan rananan-shafi da sake farawa na burauza.
- Ana ajiye fayiloli **a gida a naʼurarka** — ba a loda su zuwa wani uwar garke ba.
- Kowace burauza da naʼura suna da ajiyarsu mai zaman kansa. Zane da aka ajiye a Chrome a kwamfuta ɗaya ba ya samuwa a Firefox ko a wata na'ura.
- Sharewar bayanan shafin na burauza za ta share dukkan fayilolin da aka ajiye.

## Loda fayil kai tsaye a farawa

Idan ka buɗe KulmanLab CAD, manhajar tana loda kai tsaye **fayil ɗin da aka canza kwanan nan** daga ajiya. Ba ka bukata ka buɗe shi da hannu daga panel na Files a kowane lokaci.

## Sarrafa ajiya

Babu iyaka madaidaiciya a adadin zanen-zanen da za ka iya ajiyewa, amma ajiyar burauza tana da iyaka. Idan ka lura da faɗakarwa na ajiya, share fayiloli tsofaffi daga panel na Files.

Don cire dukkan zanen-zane da aka ajiye lokaci guda, yi amfani da umarnin [WipeStorage](../wipestorage/).

## Sunayen fayiloli da fasalin sigar

Kowace fayil tana da hoton lokaci a sunanta (misali `myplan_May22_14:30:00.dxf`). Ana ƙara wannan hoton lokaci kai tsaye idan ka [ importa](../import/) fayil, don tabbatar da cewa sake shigo da fayil na tushe iri ɗaya yana ƙirƙirar sabon shiga maimakon rufe sigar da ta gabata.
