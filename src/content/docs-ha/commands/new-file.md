# New File

Umarnin **New File** yana wanke canvas ya fara sabon zane mai tsabta. Ana ƙirƙirar sunan fayil na musamman mai hoton lokaci kai tsaye.

## Yadda za a ƙirƙiri sabon fayil

Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **New File** (alamar sabuwar shafi) a panel na File. Canvas yana wanke nan take — babu tambayoyi ko akwatin tattaunawa na tabbatarwa.

## Abin da sabon fayil ke ɗauke da shi

Fayil da aka ƙirƙira kwanan nan yana farawa da:

- **Babu abubuwa** a kan canvas.
- **Layer ɗaya na tsoho** mai suna `0` da launi fari da nauʼin layi `Continuous`.
- **Sunan fayil da aka ƙirƙira** a tsari na `kulman-Mon01_HH:MM:SS.dxf` (misali `kulman-May22_14:30:00.dxf`).

Ana ajiye fayil zuwa ajiyar burauza kai tsaye kuma yana bayyana a [Fayilolin Kwanan Nan](../files/).

## Faɗakarwa — aiki da ba a ajiye ba yana ɓacewa

Dannawa **New File** yana kawar da dukkan abubuwa a kan canvas na yanzu ba tare da faɗakarwa ba. Idan kana son riƙe zanen na yanzu, [fitar](../export/) da shi tukuna.

## Yaushe za a yi amfani da New File a maimakon Import

| Yanayi | Aiki mai shawarwari |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Farawa zane daga fara | **New File** |
| Buɗe fayil na DXF ko JSON da ke akwai | [Import](../import/) |
| Kwafin zane don yin aiki a kansa | [Fitar](../export/) da fayil na yanzu, sannan [shigo](../import/) da kwafin |

## Umarni masu alaƙa

- [Import](../import/) — buɗe zane na DXF ko JSON da ke akwai
- [Export](../export/) — sauke zanen kafin farawa sake
- [Files](../files/) — mayar da zane na baya daga ajiyar burauza
