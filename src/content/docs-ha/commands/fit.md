# Fit

Umarnin `fit` yana lissafa akwatin dubawa na dukkan abubuwa a zanen kuma yana daidaita duka matakin zoom da matsayin pan don kowace abu ta bayyana da ɗan gefe. Shine hanya mafi sauri don dawo da kallo da ya ɓace ko daidaita kanka bayan shigo da fayil na DXF.

## Daidaita kallon

Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Fit** ko rubuta `fit` a tashar umarni. Kallon yana daidaita kansa nan take kuma umarnin yana fita — babu bukatar hulɗa.

**Danna sau biyu na maɓallin tsakiya na linzamin kwamfuta** yana kunna aikin Fit iri ɗaya a kowane lokaci ba tare da kunna wani umarni ba — gajeriyar hanya mafi sauri don sake saita kallo da ya ɓace a tsakiyar zane.

## Yadda daidaitawa ta akwatin dubawa ke aiki

1. Fit yana samun akwatin dubawa mai daidaici da axis wanda ke rufe dukkan abubuwa (min X, max X, min Y, max Y).
2. Ana saita matakin zoom don girman mafi tsayi ko fadi ya cika canvas da ɗan gefe.
3. Ana ma tsakiyar kallon a kan tsakiyar akwatin dubawa.

| Yanayin zane | Sakamako |
|--------------|--------|
| Mafi fadi fiye da tsayi | Zoom an kwaɓe ta fadi |
| Mafi tsayi fiye da fadi | Zoom an kwaɓe ta tsayi |
| Abu ɗaya kaɗai | Yana daidaitawa a kewayen wannan abu kawai |
| Zane babu-kome | Kallon ba ya canzawa |

## Fit da bakan kayan aikin zoom na hannu

| | Fit | Zoom In / Zoom Out | Gilashin gilma |
|---|-----|--------------------|-------------|
| Yana ma tsakiya a | Dukkan abubuwa | Tsakiyar viewport | Mai nuni |
| Girman mataki | Kai tsaye (harbi ɗaya) | 1.5× a kowane mataki | ~1.1× a kowane tik |
| Mafi kyau don | Dawo da kallo da ya ɓace, daidaitawa bayan shigo da fayil | Matakai ciki/waje daga tsakiya | Zoom mai daidaici mai maida hankali kan mai nuni |

## Marfe na maɓallan madannai

Babu gajeriyar hanya ta madannai ga wannan umarni. Yi amfani da gajeriyar hanya ta **danna sau biyu na maɓallin tsakiya na linzamin kwamfuta** maimakon.
