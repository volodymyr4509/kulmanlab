# Zoom In

Umarnin `zoomin` yana ninka matakin zoom na yanzu da **1.5×** kuma yana fita nan take, an ma tsakiya a tsakiyar viewport. Wannan shine kamancin kayan aiki na tik ɗaya na gilashin gilma da aka zoom zuwa tsakiyar allo maimakon mai nuni.

## Ƙara girman kallon

Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Zoom In** ko rubuta `zoomin` a tashar umarni. Ana amfani da zoom nan take kuma umarnin yana fita — ba a bukatar dannawa a kan canvas.

## Yadda mataki na 1.5× ke aiki

| Zoom na yanzu | Bayan Zoom In ɗaya |
|-------------|------------------|
| 1.00× | 1.50× |
| 1.50× | 2.25× |
| 10.00× | 15.00× |
| 6,667× | 10,000× (an kwaɓe) |

Matakin zoom koyaushe ana nuna shi a **kusurwa dama-ƙasa** na canvas kusa da alamar `zoom`. Iyaka mafi girma **10,000×**; matakai ƙari ba sa yin komai.

## Zoom In na kayan aiki da gilashin gilma

| | Maɓallin Zoom In | Gilashin gilma |
|---|--------------|-------------|
| Tsakiyar zoom | Tsakiyar viewport | Matsayin mai nuni |
| Girman mataki | 1.5× ga kowace dannawa | ~1.1× ga kowane tik |
| Yana bukatar kunnawa | Aʼa | Aʼa — yana aiki koyaushe |
| Mafi kyau don | Kewayawa mai girma | Zoom mai daidaici mai maida hankali kan mai nuni |

## Marfe na maɓallan madannai

Babu gajerun hanyoyin madannai ga wannan umarni. Yi amfani da gilashin gilma maimakon — yana aiki a kowane lokaci ba tare da kunna wani umarni ba.
