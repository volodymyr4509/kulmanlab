# Leader−

Umarnin `leader-` yana cire hannu na kibiya ɗaya daga multileader da ke akwai. Ana kiyaye alamar rubutu, dogleg, da dukkan hannayen da suka rage — hannun da aka zaɓa kaɗai ake share shi. Ba za a iya cire hannu daga multileader mai hannu ɗaya kawai ba.

## Cire hannu

1. Rubuta `leader-` a tashar umarni.
2. **Danna multileader** wanda yake da hannaye biyu ko fiye. Idan leader ɗin da aka danna yana da hannu ɗaya kaɗai, tashar umarni tana nuna kuskure kuma tana jira zaɓi mai inganci.
3. **Motsa mai nuni kusa da hannun** da kake son cirewa — hannu mafi kusa an haskaka shi da alama.
4. **Danna** don cire wannan hannun.

Ana cire hannun kuma umarnin yana ci gaba da zama a aiki — za ka iya nan take danna wani leader (ko wanda iri ɗaya) don cire ƙarin hannaye. Danna **Enter**, **Space**, ko **Escape** don kammalawa.

```
  Kafin:                     Bayan:
  ◄── hannu 1                 ◄── hannu 1
       \                          \
        ●──── dogleg ──── rubutu     ●──── dogleg ──── rubutu
       /
  hannu 2 ──►  ← an cire wannan hannu
```

## Yadda ake tantance hannu mafi kusa

Umarnin yana auna nisan ta hankalta daga mai nuni zuwa sassan layin kowace hannu (ciki har da sashen daga tabon hannu na ƙarshe zuwa dogleg). Hannun da yake da mafi ƙaramin nisa an haskaka shi kuma za a cire shi a dannawa.

## Marfe na maɓallan madannai

| Maɓalli | Aiki |
|-----|--------|
| `Enter` / `Space` | Kammala cire hannaye |
| `Escape` | Soke ka sake saita |

## Bayanan ƙari

- Leader mai **hannu ɗaya kaɗai** an kiyaye shi — dole ne ka ƙara hannu tukuna kafin ka cire ɗaya.
- Ana koyaushe kiyaye matsayin dogleg da abin cikin rubutu ba tare da la'akari da wace hannu aka cire ba.
