# Redo

Umarnin `redo` yana motsawa gaba ta cikin tarihin undo, yana sake yin ayyukan da aka juya da [Undo](../undo/). Redo yana samuwa ne kawai idan ka koma baya da Undo kuma ba ka yi wani sabon canji ba tukuna.

## Yadda za a yi redo

- Rubuta `redo` a tashar umarni, ko
- Danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Redo**.

Kowace kunnawa yana sake yin aiki ɗaya da aka soke a baya. Kunna shi sau da yawa don matsawa gaba ta cikin dukkan shigarwar redo da ke akwai.

## Halin tarin redo

| Bayani | Hali |
|--------|-----------|
| Yana samuwa bayan | Mataki ɗaya ko fiye na [Undo](../undo/) |
| An wanke ta | **Kowace sabon aikin zane** — ƙarawa, gyarawa, ko share abu |
| Ajiya | Burauza, ga kowace fayil — yana wanzuwa bayan rananan-shafi (matukar babu wani sabon aiki da aka yi kafin rananan) |
| Zurfin mafi girma | Har zuwa shigarwa 20 (tafki iri ɗaya da Undo) |

Da zarar an zana, share, ko gyara sabon abu, ana wanke tarin redo kuma ba za a iya dawo da waɗannan shigarwar ba. Kawai ayyukan da aka soke waɗanda ba a maye gurbinsu da sabon aiki ba za a iya yin redo.

## Redo da Undo

| | Redo | Undo |
|---|------|------|
| Shugabanci | Matakai **gaba** ta cikin shigarwar da aka soke | Matakai **baya** ta cikin tarihi |
| Yana samuwa idan | Bayan aƙalla Undo ɗaya, ba tare da sabon aiki ba | Aƙalla aiki ɗaya da aka rikoda ya kasance |
| An wanke ta | Kowace sabon aikin zane | Babu komai |

Maɓallin Redo na kayan aiki yana mai launin toka idan babu shigarwar da za a yi redo. Yi amfani da [Undo](../undo/) tukuna don ƙirƙirar shigarwar redo.
