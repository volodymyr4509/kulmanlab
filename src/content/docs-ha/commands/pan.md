# Pan

Umarnin `pan` yana shiga yanayin ja-zuwa-scroll mai dorewa — danna ka ja a ko'ina a kan canvas don motsa kallon. Matakin zoom ba ya canzawa. Yanayin Pan yana ci gaba da zama a aiki har sai ka danna `Escape`, don haka za ka iya ja sau da yawa a kunnawa ɗaya.

## Motsa kallon

1. Rubuta `pan` a tashar umarni ko danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Pan**.
2. **Danna ka ja** a ko'ina a kan canvas don motsa kallon.
3. Sake shi ka sake ja duk sau da ake bukata.
4. Danna `Escape` don fita daga yanayin pan.

## Ja da maɓallin tsakiya — pan ba tare da kunna umarnin ba

Hanya mafi sauri ta pan: **riƙe maɓallin tsakiya na linzamin kwamfuta ka ja** a kowane lokaci, ko da wani umarni yana aiki. Babu bukatar kunna umarni. Sakin maɓallin tsakiya yana komawa zuwa yanayin da kake ciki.

## Pan da bakan zoom

| Aiki | Sakamako | Yana bukatar yanayin Pan? |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Ja da danna hagu (yanayin Pan) | Yana motsa viewport | Eh |
| Ja da maɓallin tsakiya | Yana motsa viewport | Aʼa — yana aiki koyaushe |
| Gilashin gilma | Yana zoom zuwa mai nuni | Aʼa — yana aiki koyaushe |
| Danna sau biyu na maɓallin tsakiya | Yana daidaita dukkan abubuwa | Aʼa — yana aiki koyaushe |
| [Zoom In](../zoom-in/) / [Zoom Out](../zoom-out/) | Yana matakan zoom 1.5× | Aʼa |
| [Fit](../fit/) | Yana daidaita dukkan abubuwa cikin kallo | Aʼa |

## Marfe na maɓallan madannai

| Maɓalli | Aiki |
|-----|--------|
| `Escape` | Fita daga yanayin pan |

## Shawarwari

- Yi amfani da gilashin gilma don zoom zuwa yanki na manufa, sannan yi pan don daidaita matsayi.
- **Danna sau biyu na maɓallin tsakiya na linzamin kwamfuta** yana kunna [Fit](../fit/) nan take — hanya mafi sauri don sake saita kallo da ya ɓace.
- Idan kana a tsakiyar umarni (misali, sanya ƙarshen layi), ja da maɓallin tsakiya don yin pan ba tare da soke umarnin ba.
