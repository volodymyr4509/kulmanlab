# PageManager

Umarnin `PageManager` yana buɗe akwatin tattaunawa na saitunan shafi ga layout mai aiki, yana barka ka canza tsarin takarda, shugabanci, da girma tsakanin unit na zane da millimita.

## Buɗe Page Manager

- Rubuta `PageManager` a tashar umarni, **ko**
- Danna dama a lasifikar layout a ƙasa ka zaɓi **Page Manager**.

## Saitunan

| Saitin | Bayani |
|---------|-------------|
| **Tsarin takarda** | Girman takarda na yau da kullum (A4, A3, A2, Letter, da sauransu) |
| **Shugabanci** | Portrait ko Landscape |
| **Girma** | Unit na zane ga kowace mm — yana sarrafa yadda daidaitawar model ke shiga takarda |

Canza girman takarda ko girma yana sake canza girman dukkan viewports da ke akwai a layout daidai gwargwado don matsayinsu ya ci gaba da zama daidai.

## Bayanan ƙari

- PageManager tana aiki ne kawai ga layouts na takarda. Ba ta akwai a model space ba.
- Don ƙara sabon layout, danna maɓallin **+** a jerin lasifikar a ƙasa.
- Don sake suna ko share layout, danna dama a lasifikarsa.
