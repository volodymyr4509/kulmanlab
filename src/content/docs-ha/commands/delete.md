# Delete

Umarnin `delete` yana cire abubuwan da aka zaɓa daga zanen. Ana rikodin sharewa a tarihin [Undo](../undo/) kuma ana iya sokewa har zuwa matakai 20. Babu wani akwatin tattaunawa daban na "tabbatar da sharewa" — tabbatarwa dannawar maɓalli ɗaya ce.

## Hanyoyi biyu na sharewa

**Zaɓi tukuna, sannan share** — hanya mafi sauri:

1. Zaɓi wani abu ɗaya ko fiye a kan canvas.
2. Rubuta `delete` a tashar umarni, danna maɓallin kayan aiki na **Delete**, **ko danna maɓallin `Delete`** kai tsaye.

Ana cire abubuwan nan take — babu wani mataki na tabbatarwa ƙari.

**Kunna, sannan zaɓi**:

1. Rubuta `delete` ko danna maɓallin kayan aiki (ba tare da zaɓi ba).
2. **Zaɓi abubuwa** — danna don canza zaɓi, ko ja don zaɓi ta yanki.
3. Danna **Enter**, **Space**, ko **Delete** don tabbatarwa da cire abubuwan da aka zaɓa.

## Gajeriyar hanya ta maɓallin Delete

Maɓallin `Delete` a madannai yana aiki a matsayin **gajeriyar hanya ta duniya** — idan wasu abubuwa suna zaɓaɓɓu a yanzu, danna shi yana share su nan take, ko da ba tare da buɗe umarnin Delete a tashar umarni ba. Wannan shine hanyar sharewa mafi sauri a mataki ɗaya:

```
Danna abu → danna maɓallin Delete → an gama
```

## Zaɓi a lokacin umarnin

| Hanya | Hali |
|--------|-----------|
| **Danna** | Yana canza zaɓi na abin da ke ƙarƙashin mai nuni |
| **Ja dama** (mai tsauri) | Yana zaɓen kawai abubuwan da suke cikakke cikin akwatin |
| **Ja hagu** (mai haɗuwa) | Yana zaɓen abubuwan da suke taɓa gefen akwatin |
| **Enter** / **Space** / **Delete** | Yana tabbatarwa ya share abubuwan da aka zaɓa |

## Dawo da abubuwan da aka share

Ana iya soke sharewa da umarnin [Undo](../undo/) (rubuta `undo` ko yi amfani da maɓallin kayan aiki). Ana iya soke har zuwa **matakai 20** ga kowane fayil, kuma tarihin yana ci gaba da wanzuwa a rananan-shafi. Idan ka wuce sharewa 20 ba tare da ajiyewa ba, sharewa na farko ba za a iya dawo da su ba.

## Abubuwan da ake goyon baya

Delete yana aiki akan kowane nauʼin abu — Line, Polyline, Rectangle, Circle, Arc, Ellipse, Text, Spline, Dimension, Leader, da sauransu.
