## Farfaɗowa

Idan manhajar take durkushewa a kowace farawa (misali, bayan aiki da matsayi mai girma sosai), za ka iya share dukkan bayanan da aka ajiye a gida ta ƙara `?reset` zuwa URL:

```
https://kulmanlab.com/?resetKulmanLocalStorage
```

Wannan yana share komai daga bayanan gida na burauza kuma yana farawa sabon zane mai tsabta. Sigar `?reset` tana cire kanta daga URL kai tsaye. Yi amfani da wannan a matsayin makoma ta ƙarshe idan [WipeStorage](./commands/wipestorage/) ba a iya samu ba domin manhajar ba za ta loda ko kaɗan ba.

## Yadda umarni ke aiki

Kowane umarni yana bin tsari iri ɗaya:

1. **Kunnawa** — danna maɓallin kayan aiki ko rubuta sunan umarnin a tashar umarni a ƙasan allo.
2. **Bin tambaya** — tasha tana nuna irin shigarwar da ake sa ran gaba.
3. **Kammalawa ko soke** — yawancin umarni suna kammalawa kai tsaye bayan shigarwa ta ƙarshe. Danna **Escape** a kowane lokaci don soke.

## Zaɓen abubuwa

Wasu umarnin gyara (Move, Copy, Rotate, Mirror, Scale, Delete) suna raba hali iri ɗaya na zaɓi:

- **Danna** wani abu don zaɓe shi ko soke zaɓinsa.
- **Ja dama** (daga hagu zuwa dama) don zaɓi mai tsauri — kawai abubuwan da suke cikin akwatin gaba ɗaya ake zaɓarsu.
- **Ja hagu** (daga dama zuwa hagu) don zaɓi mai haɗuwa — kowane abu da ya taɓa akwatin ana zaɓarsa.
- Danna **Enter** ko **Space** don tabbatar da zaɓi ka ci gaba zuwa mataki na gaba.
